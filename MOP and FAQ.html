The Telecom Veteran's Realization

That night, amidst the wreckage of a failed deployment, my 17 years in Telecom reliability kicked in. In 2008, if I touched a live switch without a log entry, I was fired. Why was I letting a chatbot write production code without a plan?

PART II: THE METHOD OF PROCEDURE (MOP)

The Universal AI Environment

I spent the next week building a governance layer. Not a PDF policy document, but executable code. A PowerShell framework (`install_global.ps1`) that forces the AI to behave with the discipline of a Senior Engineer.
